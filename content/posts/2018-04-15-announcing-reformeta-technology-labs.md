It gives me immense pleasure to announce the official launch of [Reformeta Technology Labs Pvt. Ltd.][1] — a software technology startup that my friend and co-founder Ruchin and I have been dreaming about, planning, and quietly building towards for the better part of this decade.

## How it began

If I had to point to a beginning, it would be somewhere around 2010–2012 — those groovy late-night conversations among friends and colleagues, where ideas flew faster than we could write them down. There was always this feeling that the two of us, with our combined experience and shared conviction, could build something meaningful.

The years that followed — 2012 through 2017 — were anything but quiet. What started as waves of thought became tides of projects, experiments, and yes, failures. We were both working full-time at our respective jobs — coming from organisations like Oracle, Adobe, and CA — and yet we never stopped. We brainstormed. We tried ideas. We failed. We learnt. And somewhere around mid-2017, we looked at each other and said: it's time to make it official.

## What is Reformeta?

The name itself is a story worth telling. The leet-code version you might see us use is **{Ref0rmTL}** — short for Reformeta Technology Labs. Ruchin comes from IIT Delhi and I'm from IIIT Hyderabad, and together we have 10+ years of product development experience between us.

Our vision is to use technology to build great systems that can quantifiably enhance the quality of life — by enabling accessibility of information and strategic indicators, with an engaging and intuitive experience. The technology spectrum we want to leverage spans AI, Machine Learning, NLP, Data Science, and HCI, with intuitive human experience as the underlying philosophy of everything we design.

## Products we are building

On the product side, we are actively working on:

- **A community platform** — connecting people who belong to a community tied by ancestry, with the ability to trace family connections and access community services

## Services we offer

Alongside our own product, we are working with clients to solve their problems. We are working with clients who want their specific problems solved — ranging from healthcare apps and e-commerce solutions to responsive websites and Progressive Web Apps. Our team offers mobile app and web app design and development services, and our technical depth also enables us to provide corporate trainings to select clients on special requirements.

Technology consulting and training serve as our early funding enablers as we grow our product portfolio.

## Where to find us

Our website is live at [reformeta.in][1]. It is early days — think of it as us chatting with you over a drink while the wiring and plumbing are still being set up. But the foundation is solid and we are very excited about what's ahead.

If you want to know more, reach out and collaborate; just say hello via any of my social handles or email address provided on contact page of Reformeta. I would love to hear from you.

Here's to the journey ahead.
