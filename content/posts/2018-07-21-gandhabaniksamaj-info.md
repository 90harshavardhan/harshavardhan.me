About the website:

gandhabaniksamaj.org is designed by me (harshachandra.org) for the people of &#8220;Gandha Banik Samaj&#8221;(https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gandhabanik) community, primarily populated in West Bengal, India. Targeted community members are spread all over the world as they have migrated to different countries over past few centuries. Primary language of communication is Bengali(https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bengali_language). Facebook group corresponding to this community is at https://www.facebook.com/groups/gandhabanik/. As of now this group has 29,000 members from all over the world and growing. Website showcases creative talents, mentions about the history, cultural background, important festivals, religious belief and famous personalities and organizations(run by the members of this community). This website also attempts to be useful by providing matrimonial ads, employment postings and community projects for the growth and development of community members.

&nbsp;

What is unique about this website:

This website is unique and interesting due to many reasons. The localized contents are in Bengali(https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bengali_language) language. This has enabled and encouraged the members of the realted community(https://www.facebook.com/groups/gandhabanik/) to produce and share localized digital contents. There aren&#8217;t many available online forums which enable and encourage the amateur talents from any particular community to produce and share their creativity and gain enough attention and praise. Contents published on this website are shared via various social sharing tools and helped the content creators establish a reputation among the viewers worldwide. This has also helped community members become more aware of their history, cultural foundations and rituals. Contents on this website are welcomed by the viewers due their personalized language experience also because viewers know that the content creators are from same community, which gives them an implicit sense of pride. Website showcases creative talents, mentions about the history, cultural background, important festivals, religious belief and famous personalities and organizations(run by the members of this community). This website also attempts to be useful by providing matrimonial ads, employment postings and community projects for the growth and development of community members. Availability of such community websites is very rare and useful. This suggests that almost all the communities of the world develop such websites.
