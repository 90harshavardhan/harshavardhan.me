Today I stumbled across an [article][1] on [TechCrunch][2].

[Anki][3] is doing some cool innovation and working on home robots — they 
named it Vector. Vector is supposed to reach closer on the parameters of 
the "Human Like" experience scale. They aren't using Alexa or Google 
Assistant; instead they used a third party knowledge graph to build the 
embedded interactive technology. The interface looks pretty cool on the 
aesthetic design front.

## Not-TL-DR Fiction

However this article led me into a thought — what if we are super advanced 
robots ourselves, created by a species which also had many ideas about its 
own inception, just like us? And at some stage, technology enabled them to 
create something which can reproduce and grow using available resources, 
continue to improve the embedded AI, and pass it on using genetic 
configuration strings.

What if this is all recursive? And continuous exploitation of resources 
leads to the change of planets — and maybe even the gas we breathe adapts 
accordingly. Somehow this doesn't appear to be a crazy idea to me.
